Salad Bowl

WARM TOFU CHOP SALAD WITH PEANUT DRESSING, for instance


Ingredients

  • ¾ cup (150g) short-grain brown rice
  • 100g (1 ½ cups) snow peas
  • 1 tablespoon neutral vegetable oil
  • 425g (1 x 15 oz) extra-firm tofu preferably pressed, cut into 3/4-inch, 2 cm cubes
  • 90g (3 cups) baby spinach, firmly packed
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • Tamari for seasoning
  • 3 carrots, shredded or cut into thin strips with a julienne peeler
  • 1 red bell pepper or yellow, chopped

Dressing

  • ¼ cup (60g) smooth peanut butter
  • 1 ½ tablespoons tamari
  • 2 teaspoons agave nectar or maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic minced or finely grated
  • 2 teaspoons fresh ginger minced or finely grated
  • 3 tablespoons water, hot

  • Sriracha sauce
  • lime wedges
  • scallions
  • roasted peanuts, chopped
  • black sesame seeds or white

Directions

  1. To make the salad, cook the brown rice as directed on this page.
  2. Meanwhile, bring a medium pot of water to boil. Add the snow peas and blanch for about 2 minutes, until bright green and crisp-tender. Drain immediately and let cool for a few minutes, then coarsely chop if desired.
  3. Heat the sesame oil in a medium nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the tofu and cook, stirring frequently, for 6 to 8 minutes, until lightly browned. Add the spinach and water and cook, stirring constantly, for about 2 minutes, until the spinach is just wilted. Stir in a splash of tamari.
  4. Transfer the tofu mixture to a large bowl. Add the rice and let cool for about 10 minutes. Add the snow peas, carrots, and bell pepper.
  5. To make the dressing, combine all the ingredients in a small bowl and whisk until evenly combined.
  6. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine. Serve with toppings of choice.
