Protein Smoothie

Peanut Butter Banana OAT SMOOTHIE, for instance


Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, large, ripe, sliced and frozen
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • ⅓ cup rolled oats, gluten-free, plus more for topping if desired
  • 3 tablespoons natural peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon flaxseed meal
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

You've noticed there's no protein powder there, but it's because you should always add protein powder to your smoothie, it's like a blender - you don't mention it, but it's a must for the preparation.

Directions

  1. Arrange the ingredients in the blender.
  2. Blend until desiredly smooth
  3. That's it.
